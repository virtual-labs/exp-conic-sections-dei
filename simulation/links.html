CONIC SECTION

  • To draw a parabola with a given distance of focus from the directrix.Also, draw a normal and a tangent at a given point from the directrix.
  • To draw an ellipse with a given distance of focus from the directrix having an eccentricity equal to 2/3.Also, draw a tangent and normal at a given point from the directrix.
  • To draw a hyperbola at a given distance of focus from the directrix having an eccentricity equal to 3/2.Also, draw a normal and a tangent at a given point from the directrix.
